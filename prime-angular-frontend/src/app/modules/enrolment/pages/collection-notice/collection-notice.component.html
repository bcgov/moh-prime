Collection Notice

The personal information you provide to the PRIME application is collected by the British Columbia Ministry of Health under the authority of s. 26(a),(c) of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act and s. 22(1)(b) of the Pharmaceutical Services Act. By clicking “Accept”, you are providing your consent to the use and disclosure of the information you submit for the purpose of:

  • Verifying your identity, assessing your eligibility and assigning privileges to the information held in the PharmaNet system;
  • ongoing access management that may include monitoring, auditing and access investigations; and
  • program evaluation and quality improvement initiatives.

Your information may be disclosed to third parties for the purposes identified above, including sites where access to PharmaNet took place (pharmacies, clinics, hospitals, health authorities, etc.), regulatory bodies or other parties as required.
