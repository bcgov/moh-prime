The Signing Authority must be the same individual for all PharmaNet access sites operating under the same organization. The Signing Authority does not need to be a PharmaNet user, a member of a regulated health profession, or providing direct patient care at the site. The Signing Authority must sign Section 5 (Certification) of the PharmaNet Access Community Practice Site Request for new PharmaNet access sites, updates to existing information for a PharmaNet access sites, discontinuing current software vendors, or discontinuations of PharmaNet access at a site.
