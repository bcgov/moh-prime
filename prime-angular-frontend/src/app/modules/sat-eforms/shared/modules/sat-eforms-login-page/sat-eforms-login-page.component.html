Healthcare professionals in BC can enrol with this application to get access to the PharmaCare Special Authority eForms application. After you enrol here, you will be able to log into PharmaCare Special Authority eForms with your BC Services Card.

If you do not have your mobile BC Services Card set up, please visit the BC Services Card website for instructions.

If you are not eligible for a BC Services Card, you can access fill-print-fax versions of the PharmaCare Special Authority forms on the PharmaCare website for prescribers.
