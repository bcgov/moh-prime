Site Registration to access PharmaNet

Signing Authority

A site must be registered by the organization’s authorized Signing Authority. If the site is owned, leased, or operated by a corporation or similar entity, the Signing Authority must be legally able to bind the corporation to the terms of the registration and the Organization Agreement for PharmaNet Use.
