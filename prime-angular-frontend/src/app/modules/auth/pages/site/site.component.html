Site Registration for PharmaNet Access

A site is the physical address that has PharmaNet access software

Signing Authority

A site must be registered by the organization’s authorized Signing Authority. The Signing Authority must be legally able to bind the corporation to the terms of the registration and the Organization Agreement for PharmaNet Use. The Signing Authority is responsible for PharmaNet activities at the site. They do not need to be a PharmaNet user.

BC Services Card

Set up your BC Services Card on your mobile device if you haven’t done so already (this is a one-time process). Once finished, come back to Site Registration and complete the online form.
