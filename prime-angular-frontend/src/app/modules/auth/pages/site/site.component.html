Site Registration for PharmaNet Access

A site is the combination of one physical location and one PharmaNet software vendor

BC Services Card

To register a site, you need to set up your BC Services Card on your mobile device. This is a one-time process.

How to set up a Mobile Card

Who can register a site?

The person legally responsible for an organization’s PharmaNet use registers the site. A site must be registered by the organization's Signing Authority. The Signing Authority is responsible for PharmaNet use, and can legally bind the organization to the terms of the registration and the Organization Agreement for PharmaNet Use.

What you need

  • Your mobile BC Services Card
  • A scan of your business licence
  • Contact details for the person(s) who oversee(s) your organization’s PharmaNet connections and related privacy, security and technical issues
