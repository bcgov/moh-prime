PRIME Request Access to PharmaNet PRIME provides approval for access to PharmaNet, but not access itself.

To get started you will need:

BC Services Card on Mobile Device
College Certification Information (for prescribers only)

PRIME Enrolment Steps

As a Health Professional working in British Columbia, use PRIME to request approval from The Ministry of Health to access PharmaNet.

BC Services Card

To get started you will need to set up your BC Services card on your mobile device.

SET UP MY BC SERVICES CARD

PRIME Enrolment

Provide contact and Health Professional information. Answer a set of self-declaration questions, then carefully read and accept the PharmaNet terms of access.

Enrolment Certificate

Once approved, to access PharmaNet, send your enrolment certificate to the PharmaNet provider used by your place of work, or to your system administrator.

BC Services Card

PRIME uses the BC Services Card to securely identify people who access PharmaNet. The Ministry of Health wants to make sure patient information on PharmaNet is accessed appropriately, only by health professionals who have a clinical need to do so.
If you do not have your BC Services Card digital credential set up on your mobile device, you will need to do that first. This is a quick process and it usually only takes a few minutes!
Visit the BC Services Card website for more information and instructions.
