PRIME Enrolment Steps

BC Services Card

To get started you will need to set up your BC Services card on your mobile device. This is a quick process and it usually only takes a few minutes.

HOW TO SET UP A MOBILE CARD

PRIME Enrolment

1. Confirm or update your contact information

2. Provide professional information where applicable

3. Answer a set of Self Declaration questions

4. Carefully read and accept the PharmaNet Terms of Access

Enrolment Certificate

Once approved, send your enrolment notification to the PharmaNet provider used by your place of work, or to your system administrator.
