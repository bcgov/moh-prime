BC Services Card

Set up your BC Services Card on your mobile device if you haven't done so already (this is a one-time process). Once finished, come back to PRIME and complete the enrolment.

How to set up a mobile card

PRIME Enrolment

Complete PRIME enrolment by answering a set of questions. It usually only takes a few minutes. If you are a registrant of a health professional College in B.C., you will need your College licence number and next renewal date.

Enrolment Notification

Once approved by the Ministry of Health, notify your PharmaNet administrator so they can set up your PharmaNet access with your vendor.
